Hospitals

Platform Power

Stella is a real-time platform built by an experienced team of engineers. Its distinguishing strength is the ability to process billions of operations in real time, drawn from any single source or a mix of disparate data sources.

Clinical Data

For hospitals, the core value is a centralized, organization-wide view of all patients, clinical activity, resources, and operational processes through a single, consistent source of truth. Stella processes and correlates data in real time across electronic health records, medical devices and bedside monitors, laboratory and imaging systems, bed and staff management platforms, and external data sources.

Patient Care

By providing instant visibility into patient status and operational processes, the platform supports real-time patient monitoring and early-warning detection, capacity and bed management, staff and resource allocation, clinical workflow coordination, regulatory compliance, and improved patient outcomes.

Smart Hospital

Stella enables hospitals to shift from reactive care to real-time operational intelligence — improving patient safety, resource efficiency, and care quality across the entire organization.

For clinical decision-making, this is positioned as decision support that surfaces information for staff, not as autonomous diagnosis.
