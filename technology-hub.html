Ultima Technology Sensing, Automation, and Process Control

The physical foundation of the data ecosystem.

Ultima Technology designs and manufactures:
  • precision sensors (temperature, pressure, vibration, flow),
  • measurement and control devices with IoT connectivity (Modbus, CAN, MQTT),
  • controllers and actuators linking physical processes to the data layer,
  • complete automation systems for industry, laboratories, energy, and transport.

Stella Systems
Real-Time Data Monitoring & Analytics

The analytical core of the ecosystem.

It acts as a universal integration and analytics engine that:
  • Connects to sensors, controllers, and automation systems (including Ultima devices),
  • analyzes incoming data with AI and predictive models,
  • triggers automatic responses and intelligent alerts,
  • integrates with business systems (CRM, ERP, MES, SCADA),
  • and enables custom dashboards and predictive workflows.

BeUp — Automation and Decision Layer

Turning analytics into intelligent business actions.

BeUp provides the automation layer that transforms Stella’s insights into real-world decisions. By integrating predictive data with CRM and workflow systems, BeUp enables processes to respond automatically — ensuring that the right actions are taken at the right time.
